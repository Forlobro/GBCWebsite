About Us

Your Gateway to Korean Innovation

GBC Jakarta (Gyeonggi Business Center Jakarta) is a non-profit organization established under the Gyeonggi Provincial Government of the Republic of Korea.

We serve as a business support platform to assist Gyeonggi-based companies in expanding their market presence in Indonesia through business matching, market entry support, trade promotion, and partnership development.
