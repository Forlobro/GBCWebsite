About Company

ROBOTECH ENG CO., LTD. is a leading Korean manufacturer specializing in the production of high-quality fans, blowers, and motors for industrial and electronic applications. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, ROBOTECH has built a strong reputation for innovation, reliability, and customer-focused solutions.

The company serves diverse sectors including HVAC systems, industrial ventilation, automotive cooling, and electronic device cooling. Their products are known for exceptional performance, energy efficiency, and durability, meeting international quality standards.

ROBOTECH is actively seeking partnerships with Indonesian distributors and manufacturers to expand their presence in Southeast Asia's growing industrial market.

Products & Services

Industrial Fans

High-capacity axial and centrifugal fans for industrial ventilation and cooling systems.

Blowers

Motors

Cooling Solutions

Custom cooling solutions for electronics, servers, and automotive applications.
