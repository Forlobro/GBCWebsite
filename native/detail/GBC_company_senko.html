About Company

SENKO is a leading Korean manufacturer specializing in advanced gas detection systems and safety equipment for industrial environments. With cutting-edge technology and a commitment to workplace safety, SENKO provides comprehensive solutions for hazardous gas monitoring and detection.

The company's products are designed to protect workers and facilities from dangerous gas leaks, ensuring compliance with international safety standards. SENKO's gas detectors feature real-time monitoring, instant alerts, and reliable performance in the most demanding industrial conditions.

SENKO is seeking partnerships with Indonesian distributors, safety equipment suppliers, and industrial facility managers to expand their presence in Southeast Asia's growing industrial safety market.

Products & Services

Fixed Gas Detectors

Permanent installation gas detection systems for continuous monitoring of industrial facilities.

Portable Gas Detectors

Handheld multi-gas detectors for personal safety and confined space entry.

Gas Monitoring Systems

Centralized monitoring solutions with real-time data logging and alarm management.

Safety Sensors

High-precision sensors for detecting toxic, combustible, and oxygen-deficient gases.
